Indicator name:
Solar ultraviolet (UV) radiation exposure
Short name:
UV radiation
Topic:
Risk factors
Rationale:
As part of a broader project to assess major risk factors to health, the burden of disease resulting from exposure to ultraviolet (UV) radiation was assessed.  
Definition:
The estimates presented are population-weighted average daily ambient UVR level (in J/m2) for the years 1997–2003 for selected countries.  
Method of estimation:
The estimations of UVR for this burden of disease assessment involve assuming a population-level  exposure represented by annual ambient  erythemally weighted UVR (calculated from  satellite data) or a proxy such as latitudinal  position.

Special facility surveys
