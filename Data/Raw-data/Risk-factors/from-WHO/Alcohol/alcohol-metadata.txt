Indicator name:
Alcohol, average daily intake in grams among drinkers
Short name:
Grams of pure alcohol per day
Topic:
Risk factors
Definition:
Grams of pure alcohol per day is a measure of alcohol consumption.
Method of estimation:
Given the specific weight of alcohol of 0.793 g/cm3 (at 20 °C), per capita consumption in litres of pure ethanol per year can be converted into grams per day as follows: g/day = APC x 1000 x 0.793/365 days
